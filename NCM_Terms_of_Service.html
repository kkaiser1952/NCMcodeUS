Net Control Manager (NCM) - Terms of Service

1. Introduction

Welcome to the Net Control Manager (NCM) application, designed to streamline Amateur Radio Net check-ins, manage net resources, and make net reporting easier and more efficient. By using this app, you agree to comply with these Terms of Service.

2. Usage Guidelines

NCM is designed for use with all U.S.-based Amateur Radio Callsigns and limited GMRS Callsigns. Please ensure that your usage of this app is legal under the laws governing your location and operation. If an action is illegal, do not use this app for that purpose.

3. Prohibited Uses

You agree not to use the NCM app for any unlawful or prohibited activities, including but not limited to:

  • Engaging in illegal activities.

  • Spamming or any form of unauthorized communication.

5. Data Collection and Privacy Policy

Data Collected: The NCM app collects the following information:

  • Callsign
  • Full Name
  • Latitude/Longitude
  • Email (if provided)
  • More depending on usage (see the help file)

Data Usage: The collected data is stored and used to ensure the safety and coordination of individuals during nets and/or deployments.

Data Protection: All known (to me) methods of protecting the collected data have been implemented to safeguard your information. While every effort is made to secure your data, no method of transmission over the internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure.
